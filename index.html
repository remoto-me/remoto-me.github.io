A Letter from our CEO

Save yourself. Save the world.

Life is short and the world is warming. What can you do about it? I bet you already are: Remoto Remote working.

The nature of work has changed from a place you used to go to something you do with your mobile or laptop from anywhere.

Still, it is hard to keep everybody in sync when people don't bump into each other at the water-cooler. And even for people that meet in the office, effective communication doesn't just happen. How can you maintain coherent customer communication over email, phone or messaging apps, involving several people in your organization?

This is a problem I myself have had, along with several professionals we interviewed. Remoto's goal is to allow you to communicate better with your customers and your team, and prove that people don't need to be in the same room to be on the same page.

Remoto is more than a work tool, it is a way of thinking about work. Think outside the cubicle, be Remoto.

Yours,

Fred Guth, Founder & CEO
